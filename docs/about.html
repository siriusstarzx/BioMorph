About

BioMorph is a Cell Painting - Cell Health dervied featture space designed to enhance the interpretability of cell morphological features. With a commitment to advancing the field of cell morphology analysis, this initiative curates a comprehensive re-organization of data that incorporates biological meaning to CellProfiler features.

Built on the Cell Painting dataset, BioMorph dataset encompasses a vast array of biolgoically relevant profiles or features for 30,000+ compounds. The resource is made publicly available to researchers to assesses the performance of a variety of machine learning models in predicting endpoints with the BioMorph dataset.

For referencing, please cite:
