Welcome

BioMorph_Space is a benchmark specially designed for enhancing the interpretability of cell morphological features. It aims to provide a platform for analyzing the role of these features in various cell health phenotypes. The system is built upon a combination of Cell Painting assays and comprehensive Cell Health assays. We present the BioMorph space, a transformative method to gain deeper insights into phenotypic characteristics and cellular processes.
